## Directory Summary
The `data` directory contains the output of the synthetic data generation engine. These CSV files follow the ScholarConnect database schema and are populated with realistic records for students, faculty, and academic courses, including their complex relationships and evaluations.

## Dataset Index
- `Students.csv` — 80 records of synthetic student data.
- `Professors.csv` — 12 records of synthetic professor data.
- `Courses.csv` — Complete catalogue of 25 courses.
- `TeachingAssignments.csv` — Mapping of professors to their assigned courses.
- `Enrollments.csv` — Student enrollment records across various courses.
- `ProfessorEvaluations.csv` — Anonymous feedback and ratings for faculty.
- `EvaluationComments.csv` — Qualitative comments associated with professor evaluations.
